Earth at Night

The lights of human activity sparkle across the dark planet, tracing roads and political boundaries, revealing areas where use of electric power is expanding and those where storms have knocked out power.

North Korea appears dark, a stark contrast to the bright, densely-populated countries surrounding it, and brightly-lit fishing fleets stand out in the black oceans. Do you see any light or dark spots where you didn't expect them?
